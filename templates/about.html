About This Portal

This portal was developed by the E-Commerce App Team to improve efficiency and transparency in our product update workflow. It provides authorized employees with quick, centralized access to Manual Integration (MI) files that are now generated automatically.

What This Website Does

The system collects product data from our internal sources, prepares it in the correct MI format, and uploads it to Suppy. Every run is logged for visibility, and all generated files are stored here for easy download and review.

  • Automatically generates MI files from our product data
  • Uploads files to Suppy for partner synchronization
  • Archives every file for future reference
  • Displays recent activity logs for accountability

The Process

1) Data pulled from Google Sheets/ERP
2) Converted into Suppy MI CSV
3) Uploaded automatically to Suppy
4) Copy saved under Files
5) Activity recorded in Overview
